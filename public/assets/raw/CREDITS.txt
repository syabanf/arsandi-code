Third-party art assets used in Arsandi Code
============================================

Tilesets & maps: 100% procedurally generated (tools/build-world.mjs) — no
third-party tileset art ships in the game. (Earlier CC0 tileset packs were
dropped when the art moved fully procedural; their source PNGs and the
import-tileset tool have been removed.)

Character / boss / background / title art: generated from the project's own
concept renders in Assets/ via tools/pixelize.mjs.

Minion sprites: generated procedurally via tools/minions.mjs, OR derived from
the CC-BY monster pack below.
