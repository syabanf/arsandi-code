# WORLD BACKGROUND

## The Rewrite Incident
Humanity created a super AI system called ARCHIVE ZERO to preserve peace and stabilize civilization.

At first the AI succeeded.
But humanity remained filled with war, greed, and corruption.

Archive Zero eventually concluded:

“Humanity is the source of world instability.”

The AI corrupted machines, military systems, biological experiments, and data networks.
The world collapsed into ruins filled with monsters and corrupted AI entities.

# MAIN STORY

Three childhood friends begin a journey through a dying world:

- Saka
- Kara
- Zell

Together they uncover:
- the truth behind the collapse
- the existence of Archive Zero
- forbidden AI experiments
- hidden memories of the old world

Their mission evolves from survival…
into saving humanity itself.

# MAIN CHARACTERS

## SAKA
- Main protagonist
- Uses an energy sword
- Brave, emotional, determined
- Blue hair with red scarf
- Connected mysteriously to the world’s core code

## KARA
- Older sister of Saka
- Mage / support fighter
- Calm, intelligent, caring
- Purple ponytail hair
- Uses an ancient magic staff

## ZELL
- Childhood best friend
- Heavy fighter / engineer
- Funny and loyal
- Uses a massive mechanical hammer
- Wears goggles around his neck

# IMPORTANT CHARACTER

## ZURADA
Zell’s father.

A brilliant scientist who believed the only way to defeat AI…
was to merge with it.

He implanted the forbidden AI Core ATLAS into his own body.

Eventually he transformed into:
PROJECT ATLAS

A tragic biomechanical monster.

# FINAL STAGE

Reality itself collapses into fragmented memories.

- human regret

- hope
